ABOUT US

ABOUT US

Furnid is an Indonesian furniture company with over 20 years of experience in bringing comfort to people's homes all around the world. We bring the Furnid brand to millions of homes worldwide, providing well-designed, useful, and durable furnishing solutions to those who want comfort in their home. We're fascinated by the world around us and want to positively impact people's lives. We want everyone to feel more comfortable in their own home when they use our items.

"Providing Convenience to Every Home Around the World" is our company's goal. Most people spend 80 percent or even 100 percent of their time at home in a single day. As a result, having a comfy house is crucial. Comfortable furniture is one of the determinants of home comfort. As a furniture company, we recognize that comfortable furniture is essential in establishing a comfortable home; as a result, each of our furniture items is handcrafted by highly skilled furniture artisans using the finest materials to create the best furniture products for everyone across the world.
